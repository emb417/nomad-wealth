    üèõÔ∏è Architecture Overview

    Nomad Wealth is a policy-driven Monte Carlo simulation framework for financial planning.
    Its architecture is designed for audit clarity, transparency, and extensibility, ensuring that every projection, chart, and policy is IRS-aligned and defensible.

    üîç High-Level Design

    The system is organized into modular components under src/:

    • Entry Point (app.py) ‚Üí orchestrates configuration loading, staging, parallel execution, aggregation, and visualization.
    • Forecast Engine (forecast_engine.py) ‚Üí runs the monthly simulation loop, applying transactions, policies, market returns, and taxes.
    • Buckets & Holdings (buckets.py) ‚Üí core data structures representing accounts, holdings, and asset classes.
    • Policies (policy_engine.py, policies_transactions.py) ‚Üí enforce refill rules, liquidation hierarchies, and penalty logic.
    • Transactions (rules_transactions.py) ‚Üí define fixed, recurring, salary, Social Security, Roth conversions, and other flows.
    • Economic Factors (economic_factors.py) ‚Üí simulate inflation and market returns using historical distributions.
    • Taxes (taxes.py) ‚Üí apply IRS-compliant tax rules for ordinary income, capital gains, and Social Security.
    • Visualization (visualizations.py) ‚Üí generate interactive charts (historical, per‚Äëtrial, Monte Carlo) and CSV/HTML exports for audit clarity.
    • Helper Functions (app.py) ‚Üí utilities for timing, inflation modifiers, and bucket creation.

    üìÇ Data Flow

    1. Configuration Loading

    2. JSON files in config/ define buckets, policies, tax brackets, and simulation parameters.

    3. CSV files in data/ seed historical balances and define fixed/recurring transactions.

    4. Staging

    5. stage_load() ‚Üí loads JSON configs and CSV inputs.

      • Inputs include:
      • balances.csv ‚Üí seed balances.
      • fixed.csv ‚Üí one‚Äëtime events.
      • recurring.csv ‚Üí ongoing monthly flows.
      • Policies (policies.json), tax brackets (tax_brackets.json), inflation (inflation_rates.json + thresholds + gain table), and marketplace premiums (marketplace_premiums.json) are also loaded.
    6. stage_prepare_timeframes() ‚Üí builds historical (hist_df) and future (future_df) frames.

    7. stage_init_components() ‚Üí seeds buckets, policies, inflation, tax calculator, market gains, and transactions.

      • Helper functions ensure integrity:
      • create_bucket() ‚Üí allocates balances across holdings, correcting rounding drift.
      • seed_buckets_from_config() ‚Üí validates bucket definitions against historical balances.
      • build_description_inflation_modifiers() ‚Üí applies inflation profiles consistently across categories.
      • timed() ‚Üí logs performance metrics for reproducibility.

    8. Parallel Simulation Loop (Forecast Engine)

    9. Monte Carlo trials executed in parallel with ProcessPoolExecutor.

    10. Each trial applies monthly transactions, refill policies, market returns, and taxes.

    11. FlowTracker records all debits/credits for audit clarity.

    12. Aggregation & Summary

    13. Trial results aggregated into DataFrames for net worth, taxes, taxable balances, and monthly returns.

    14. Property liquidation events tracked across simulations.

    15. Taxable balances at SEPP end month recorded for compliance checks.

    16. Visualization Layer

    17. Per-trial charts: monthly expenses, transactions, taxes, forecasts.
    18. Aggregate Monte Carlo charts: monthly returns, taxable balances, totals/rates, net worth distribution.
    19. Historical charts: bucket gains and net worth trajectory.
    20. All charts exportable to HTML and CSV for reproducibility.

    üßæ IRS Compliance

    The tax engine enforces IRS rules with explicit, layered logic:

    • Ordinary income brackets inflated annually.
    • Capital gains layered above ordinary income.
    • Social Security capped at 85% of provisional income.
    • Penalty tax applied only when flagged in metadata.
    • AGI includes salary, withdrawals, conversions, gains, and Social Security.
    • Taxable income calculated after deductions.
    • Inflation modeled stochastically, anchored to a base year.

    üìä Visualization Integration

    Visualization is embedded in the simulation loop:

    • Historical context ‚Üí charts of bucket gains and net worth trends.
    • Per-trial transparency ‚Üí expenses, transactions, taxes, forecasts.
    • Aggregate clarity ‚Üí distributions of returns, balances, taxes, and net worth across Monte Carlo trials.
    • Audit reproducibility ‚Üí all charts exportable to HTML/CSV with timestamped filenames, with consistent color palettes, percentile overlays, and hover text for interpretability.

    üéØ Design Principles

    • Policy-First ‚Üí all financial rules are declarative JSON, transparent and repeatable.
    • Audit Clarity ‚Üí every dollar is traceable across buckets and scenarios.
    • Extensibility ‚Üí modular design allows new transaction types, policies, or tax rules.
    • Resilience ‚Üí Monte Carlo sampling embraces volatility, quantifying sufficiency and optimization.
    • Parallelism ‚Üí simulations scale efficiently with ProcessPoolExecutor.
    • Integrity ‚Üí helper functions enforce balance correctness, inflation consistency, and reproducible timing.
